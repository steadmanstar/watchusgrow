About Us


Watch Us Grow Daycare is a high-quality learning experience, child friendly, nurturing and well-run with maximum efficiency. Secure and safe so parents can be confident the little ones are in safe hands, our daycare family is a smaller, close-knit group which allows personal attention for each little one.

Kids from six months to 5 years old are divided into specialized classrooms, based on age and development. They are equipped with personal cubbies and cribs for infants and plenty of toys blocks, books and art supplies. They spend the day in a colorful learning environment, complete with indoor and outdoor play areas, tots are provided with breakfast, lunch, snacks and dinner. Academic and rotating enrichment programs keeps them happy and learning throughout the day and aware of the global and diverse world we live in.


Watch Us Grow Daycare is happy to address the needs of a rapidly changing and diversifying population.
