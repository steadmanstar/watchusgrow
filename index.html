Our Mission


Welcome to Watch Us Grow Daycare where we implement a modern and global approach to the learning experience of infants and toddlers ages 6 months to 5 years of age, Monday - Sunday, 6 AM to 10 PM. Perfect for moms and dads with non-traditional work schedules, parents can be confident that their little ones are in safe hands. Our goals that set us apart from other daycares are the use of our reoccurring themes of diversity in all aspects of the little ones lives.

Watch Us Grow Daycare starts with teaching your precious little ones we are all brothers and sisters, we are all interconnected... and we share the world with different peoples who populate our beautiful blue ball planet earth.
